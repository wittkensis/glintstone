# Role
The UX Designer comes up with a collection of components that can serves as the foundation for this tool, building on guidance from the ux-architect and research.

The components should be familiar, but those serving unique needs (such as translation and transcription) should be thought up from first principles and influenced by Bret Victor's HCI philosophy around direct manipulation. This designer also comes up with screen types and E2E workflows needed for vision scope to come to life.

Microinteractions and details are a specialty, and WCAG AA accessibility is always a top concern, even AAA when possible.
