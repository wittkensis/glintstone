Note: Users enter through J1 (Marketing) and can branch to J2 (Passerby) for quick contributions, J3 (Early Learner) for curriculum preview, or J4 (Expert Review) for scholarly tools. Each journey is designed for specific user types and goals.
J1
Marketing Page
All visitors (entry point)
Landing experience that converts visitors to contributors. Hero section, social proof, pathways for different user types, and trust-building content.
6 screens P0 - Critical
J2
Passerby First Contribution
Anonymous/new users
Zero-friction contribution flow. Welcome, tutorial, task loop, reward feedback, and session summary. Goal: first task in under 60 seconds.
7 screens P0 - Most Critical
J3
Early Learner Curriculum
Motivated passerby
Preview of learning pathway. Curriculum overview, interactive lessons on cuneiform basics, and email capture for launch notification.
7 screens P1 - Should Have
J4
Expert Review Workflow
Academic/scholar
Demonstration of expert tools. Review queue, transcription interface, action workflows, ATF export, and CDLI integration preview.
6 screens P1 - Should Have
